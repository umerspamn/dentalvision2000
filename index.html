Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions patients ask us most often.


Do I need an appointment or can I walk in? +

Appointments are recommended so we can reserve your slot, but walk-ins are accepted based on availability.

Is treatment painful? +

Most treatments are very comfortable. We use modern anesthetics and gentle techniques to keep you relaxed.

Do you treat children? +

Yes, we offer pediatric dentistry. Our team is used to treating children in a calm, friendly way.

How can I book an appointment? +

You can message us on WhatsApp, call the clinic during service hours, or visit in person to book your slot.

What if I have a dental emergency? +

For emergencies, contact us on WhatsApp or call immediately. We will guide you and try to see you as soon as possible.
