What is Root Canal Treatment?

A root canal treatment is a way to save a tooth that is badly infected or very painful, instead of removing it. The inside of the tooth is gently cleaned, disinfected and sealed so that the pain and infection do not return.

At DentalVision2000, we focus on comfort. The area is numbed properly first, and the dentist works step by step while explaining what they are doing in simple language.

When do you need this treatment?

  • Strong, lasting toothache, especially at night.
  • Pain when chewing or biting on a tooth.
  • Tooth feels very sensitive to hot or cold drinks.
  • Swelling or a small pimple near the tooth on the gums.
  • A tooth that has turned darker in colour after an injury.

How we diagnose your case

First, the dentist listens to your complaint and checks your teeth and gums. We may take an X-ray of the tooth to see the roots and the infection clearly. After this, the dentist explains the condition, all possible options and the expected cost before starting any treatment.

How the treatment works

  1. We numb the tooth and surrounding area so you stay comfortable.
  2. A small opening is made on the tooth to reach the infected nerve.
  3. The inside is cleaned and shaped carefully, then rinsed with special solutions.
  4. The cleaned space is filled and sealed, and the tooth is later covered with a filling or crown.

Benefits of Root Canal Treatment

  • Relieves strong tooth pain and infection.
  • Helps you keep your own natural tooth for many years.
  • Allows normal chewing on that side again.
  • Prevents the infection from spreading to nearby teeth or jaw.
  • Usually more affordable than removing the tooth and replacing it later.
